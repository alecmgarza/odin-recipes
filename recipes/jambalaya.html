Jambalaya

Description

Ingredients

  • peanut oil
  • cajun seasoning
  • andouille sausage
  • boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • diced onions
  • diced green peppers
  • diced celery
  • garlic cloves
  • crushed tomatoes
  • red pepper flakes
  • ground black pepper
  • salt
  • hot pepper sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • file powder
  • white rice
  • chicken broth

Steps

  1. Heat peanut oil in a pan.
  2. Add Cajun seasoning to sausage and chicken. Saute sausage until brown.
  3. Remove sausage and set aside.
  4. Add peanut oil and saute chicken pieces until lightly browned.
  5. Remove chicken and set aside.
  6. Saute onion, green pepper, celery, and garlic until tender. Stir in crushed tomatoes and season with red pepper, black pepper, salt, hot pepper sauce, Wrcestershire sauce, and file powder.
  7. Stir in to chicken and sausage, cook for 10 minutes.
  8. Stir in rice and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 25 minutes.
