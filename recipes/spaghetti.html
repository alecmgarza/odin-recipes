Spicy Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • spagetti noodles
  • marinara pasta sauce
  • ground beef
  • salt
  • green pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  3. Drop noodles into the boiling water, leave for 12-15 minutes.
  4. While noodles are cooking, brown ground beef in a pan.
  5. Dice green pepper and add to ground beef, add more peppers for spice if needed.
  6. Pour pasta sauce over ground beef, stir until hot.
  7. Drain water from noodles after 12-15 minutes are up.
  8. Pour meatsauce over noodles and serve.
